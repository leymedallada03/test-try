System Info

Version 1.0 • 2026

Mission

To provide accurate, timely, and accessible household data that supports disaster risk reduction initiatives and enhances community resilience in Alitagtag, Batangas.

Vision

A disaster-resilient Alitagtag where every household is accounted for, prepared, and protected through efficient data management and responsive governance.

Core Values

  • Integrity - Accurate and truthful data
  • Service Excellence - Committed to residents
  • Accountability - Responsible data handling
  • Innovation - Continuous improvement
  • Community-Centered - People first

Our Commitment

The Purok Kaligtasan 133 system is committed to protecting every household's data while ensuring that no family is left behind during disasters. We pledge to use this information responsibly and solely for the safety and welfare of the Alitagtag community.

Guiding Philosophy

"Accurate data today saves lives tomorrow."

Every household record entered into this system represents a family that depends on us for timely disaster response and effective risk reduction planning.

Service Pledge

  • Respond to data requests within 24 hours
  • Maintain 99.9% system uptime
  • Update household records in real-time
  • Ensure data privacy and confidentiality

Our Motto

"Kaligtasan ng bawat tahanan, responsibilidad nating lahat."

(Safety of every home is everyone's responsibility.)
